    📍 Start: TKTS Red Steps (Father Duffy Square, 47th St) — elevated red bleacher steps with sweeping view over all of Times Square. Best photo spot.
    👟 Walk south on Broadway → Times Square bowtie (44th–46th St) — the heart of the square. Giant LED billboards, street performers, full energy.
    👟 Continue south → 42nd St — Restaurant Row — dozens of restaurants and fast food. Good spot for a late dinner or dessert.
    👟 Walk east 2 blocks on 42nd St → Bryant Park (42nd & 6th Ave, 5 min) — beautiful lit-up green space at night, free, family friendly.
    👟 Walk north on 5th Ave → New York Public Library (42nd & 5th, 2 min from Bryant Park) — stunning Beaux-Arts façade, great for photos at night.
    👟 Continue north on 5th Ave → Rockefeller Center (49th & 5th, 10 min walk) — iconic Art Deco plaza, NBC Studios, Top of the Rock if you want a night view alternative to Empire State.
    👟 Walk west back to Broadway → Broadway Theater District (44th–52nd St) — marquee lights of Hamilton, Lion King, Chicago and more. No ticket needed to admire the buzz.
    🕐 Total walking loop ~1.5 miles · About 45–60 min at a relaxed pace · All flat, well-lit, and safe at night
